About this application

Angular 2 application celebrating the men and women of the NASA space programs. Future dataset additions will include members of international space programs and contemporary members of the aerospace community. Current dataset is somewhat arbitrary based on what I've been reading, such as, Failure is Not An Option by Gene Kranz and The Right Stuff by Tom Wolfe. I did my best to be accurate; however, apologies in advance for any errors or typos I may have made in the data.

Purpose is to demonstrate an intuitive and responsive interface for searching and filtering through related collections of data.

- 2016 Thomas Rundle
